About Us

Mangata and Gallo is a jewelry store that specializes in special
occasions like engagements, weddings and anniversaries.
We primarily operate online and have a storefront in
Toronto, Ontario with an atelier attached for browsing.

Mangata & Gallo's selection of jewelry is known for
its high-quality and classic look.
